This Is Me

Web Designer turned Web Developer. Always looking to learn.

For as long as I can remember I’ve wanted to know how audiences interact with the world around them and throughout my early education this was primarily focused on design and digital media. Over the years, this infatuation has focused more on interaction and web technologies, cultivating my thirst for finding out how they work and how to take advantage of their capabilities.

After completing, and during, my degree I worked for design agency that allowed me to begin my journey into the development world and knowing that this is where I wanted to be, I took the decision to expand my coding knowledge, undertaking the Web Development Immersive course at GA.

I’m looking for a fast pace environment with continuous learning opportunities. I am no stranger to hard work and late nights (coding...) and relish getting my teeth into a big project that tests my skills to the limit, giving me the opportunity find new ways of working.
